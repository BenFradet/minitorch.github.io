We do this with the following algorithm:

  1. Initialize a queue with a the final variable and derivative.

1. While the queue is not empty, pull a variable and derivative from the queue. 2.

  1. If the variable is a leaf (is_leaf) then add its derivative to .derivative (_add_deriv) and loop to (1).

  2. If the variables is not a leat (is_non_leaf) call .chain_rule on its history to propagate its derivative.

3. Loop through all the variables+derivative produced by chain rule. 4.

  1. If the variable is in the queue (check .name), add the new deriv to the previous deriv.

  2. Otherwise add to the queue.
